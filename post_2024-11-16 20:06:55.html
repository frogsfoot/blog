it's been a little crazy just how much having a social life relies on joining a society. I'm pretty lucky because I joined and gelled with the drama society immediately, but all those horror stories about people being super lonely for all of first year always seem scarily close to happening. one of my tumblr mutuals reached out and said that joining the board game soc saved their life and it's like. yeah, I don't know how I'd be coping if I wasn't being forced to eat with and talk to people at least twice (usually 3) times a week. in fact, our leading man (who is the friendliest and most extroverted man) told me that our post rehearsal pub trips are his entire social life. spooky. Speaking of drama soc, we have a game called 'spotted!' that has been going on for probably 8 years, where if you see another member of the society in the wild, you have to take a photo of them and send it in the group chat without them seeing you. and despite the fact i am a pretty active member of the soc and know almost everyone, and i've gone to most of the socials AND go into town every day, I have avoided being spotted so far. and it is driving me a little insane. my doom is coming and I can smell it. I've had a couple near misses, where I've seen people try to take a photo of me [therefore disqualifying them], or received texts along the lines of "YOU BASTARD I NEARLY HAD YOU" just as I've walked into a seminar room. but no actual spots yet. my days are numbered.
